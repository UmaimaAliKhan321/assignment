Full Body Strength

This workout targets all major muscle groups, helping you build strength and endurance.

Squats: 3 sets x 12 reps

Push-Ups: 3 sets x 15 reps

Plank: 3 sets x 30 seconds

Upper Body Circuit

Boost your upper body strength with this circuit focused on arms, shoulders, and chest.

Dumbbell Curls: 3 sets x 12 reps

Tricep Dips: 3 sets x 10 reps

Shoulder Press: 3 sets x 12 reps

Core and Abs

This core-focused routine helps build abdominal strength and stability.

Crunches: 3 sets x 15 reps

Leg Raises: 3 sets x 12 reps

Russian Twists: 3 sets x 20 reps (10 each side)
